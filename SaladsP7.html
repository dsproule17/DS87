                                                        Danny's Pizzeria Salads

Each Type of Salads

At Danny's Pizzeria, we offer four delicious salads, each packed with fresh ingredients and bold flavors. Our "Caesar Salad" features crisp romaine, crunchy croutons, and parmesan cheese, all tossed in creamy caesar dressing. For a Mediterranean touch, our "Greek Salad" combines fresh cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, olives, and feta cheese with a zesty dressing. Our "Italian Salad" is a flavorful mix of crisp greens, cherry tomatoes, red onions, olives, and shredded cheese, drizzled with a tangy italian dressing. And for a hearty, creamy option, our "Potato Salad" is made with tender potatoes and a rich, flavorful dressing. Whether as a side or a meal on its own, our salads are the perfect fresh addition to your dining experience!

Our Caesar Salad

Our "Caesar Salad" is a classic favorite, made with crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and freshly grated parmesan cheese, all tossed in a rich and creamy caesar dressing. The perfect blend of textures and flavors, this salad is a deliciously refreshing side or a satisfying meal on its own. Pair it with your favorite pizza for a fresh and flavorful combination!

Our Greek Salad

Our "Greek Salad" is a fresh and flavorful Mediterranean classic. Made with crisp cucumbers, juicy tomatoes, red onions, olives, and creamy feta cheese, it's tossed in a zesty dressing that brings everything together. Light, refreshing, and packed with bold flavors, this salad is the perfect side or a delicious meal on its own!

Our Italian Salad

Our "Italian Salad" is a vibrant mix of crisp lettuce, cherry tomatoes, red onions, black olives, and shredded cheese, all tossed in a tangy italian dressing. Bursting with fresh flavors and a perfect balance of crunch and zest, this salad makes a refreshing side or a tasty, light meal. It’s the perfect complement to any pizza or pasta dish!

Our Potato Salad

Our "Potato Salad" is a creamy and hearty classic, made with tender, perfectly cooked potatoes tossed in a rich and flavorful dressing. Blended with just the right seasonings, it’s smooth, satisfying, and the perfect side to complement any meal. Whether you're enjoying it with pizza or on its own, this comforting dish is always a crowd-pleaser!
