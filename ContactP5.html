How to Contact Us!

We’d love to hear from you! For immediate assistance, placing an order, or making a reservation, you can call us directly at 705-725-5959. If you prefer ordering online, simply visit our website to place an order for pickup or delivery. For inquiries, feedback, or special requests, feel free to email us at DannysPizzeria@gmail.ca, and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible. You can also connect with us through social media on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. If you're nearby, we’d love for you to stop by our pizzeria at 100 Pizza Street, Barrie, ON — our team is always happy to assist you in person.

   What To Expect From Us

When you reach out to Danny's Pizzeria, you can expect a friendly, professional, and prompt response every time. Whether you’re calling us on the phone or sending an email, our team is dedicated to providing top-notch customer service. If you call, we’ll answer with a warm greeting and be ready to assist with any orders, inquiries, or reservations. We take the time to listen to your needs and ensure you're satisfied, whether you're placing a custom order or asking about our menu. No matter how you contact us, you can always count on clear, helpful communication and a welcoming attitude!
